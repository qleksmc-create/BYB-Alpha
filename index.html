GENERAL

What is BYB Alpha?
▼
BYB Alpha is an algorithmic trading platform providing ready-made Expert Advisor (EA) bots for MetaTrader 4/5, cTrader, and other platforms. We also offer custom bot development through AlphaForge, where our developers code your exact trading strategy from scratch.
Do I need coding experience to use the bots?
▼
No coding knowledge is required. Our bots come with step-by-step video and written installation guides. If you can navigate MetaTrader, you can run a BYB Alpha bot. Our support community is always available to help.
What trading pairs does the bot work on?
▼
Unwicked Lite covers EUR/USD and GBP/USD. Unwicked Pro expands to 12+ forex pairs plus gold. Forge Apex covers all major forex pairs, gold (XAUUSD), indices (NAS100, US30), and crypto. Custom AlphaForge bots can target any market your broker offers.
Is algorithmic trading legal?
▼
Yes, algorithmic trading is legal in most jurisdictions. However, regulations vary by country and broker. Always verify that your broker permits automated trading (EAs) and that you comply with local financial regulations. We cannot provide legal advice.

PRICING & LICENSING

Are there any recurring fees?
▼
No. All bot purchases are one-time payments. You own the software outright. No monthly subscriptions, no hidden fees, no profit-sharing. Future updates for Pro and Apex tiers are included at no extra charge.
Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
▼
Yes. All bot products come with a 30-day no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within 30 days of purchase, contact our support team and we'll process your full refund immediately.
How many accounts can I run the bot on?
▼
Lite licences cover 1 live account. Pro licences cover up to 3 live accounts. Apex licences cover unlimited live accounts. AlphaForge custom bots are licensed per project.
What payment methods do you accept?
▼
We accept all major debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDT), and bank transfer. All payments are processed securely via SSL encryption.

SETUP & INSTALLATION

How long does it take to set up?
▼
Most users are up and running within 15–30 minutes. After purchase, you receive an instant download link plus access to full setup documentation, video walkthroughs, and our Discord community.
Do I need to keep my computer on 24/7?
▼
We strongly recommend a VPS for 24/5 uninterrupted operation. Full documentation is provided for setting up on BeeksFX, Forex VPS, AWS, and Azure. Most professional traders use a VPS.
Which brokers are compatible?
▼
BYB Alpha bots work with any broker supporting MetaTrader 4/5 or cTrader. We recommend ECN/STP brokers with tight spreads. A recommended broker list is in our Discord documentation channel.

PERFORMANCE & RESULTS

Are the performance results real?
▼
Yes. All performance data is sourced from live trading accounts independently verified on Myfxbook — the industry standard for third-party trade verification. We never use curve-fitted backtests in our marketing.
Is past performance guaranteed in the future?
▼
No. As with all trading, past performance is not a guarantee of future results. Markets change, and any trading system can experience losing periods. Only trade with capital you can afford to lose.
What is the minimum account size recommended?
▼
We recommend a minimum of $500 for Lite, $1,000 for Pro, and $2,500 for Apex to allow for proper risk management and position sizing. Smaller accounts will have smaller position sizes.

SECURITY & PRIVACY

Does the bot need access to my funds?
▼
BYB Alpha bots run locally on your machine or VPS within MetaTrader or cTrader. We never have access to your broker account, passwords, or funds. The bot operates through the platform's API — same as your manual trades.
Is my personal data safe?
▼
Yes. We collect only information necessary to process your order. We never sell or share your data with third parties. All data is encrypted and handled in accordance with GDPR and applicable data protection regulations.
